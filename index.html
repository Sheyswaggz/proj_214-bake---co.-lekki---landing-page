Our Story

A passion for baking that started in a small Lagos kitchen and grew into Lekki's favourite bakery

Founded in 2016, Bake & Co. began with a simple dream — to bring the joy of freshly baked goods to the heart of Lekki.

What started as weekend baking sessions in a tiny kitchen has blossomed into a beloved neighbourhood bakery. Every morning, our team of passionate bakers arrives before dawn to prepare the day's offerings, using only the finest locally-sourced ingredients and time-honoured recipes passed down through generations.

We believe that great baking is an art — it requires patience, quality ingredients, and most importantly, love. From our signature sourdough bread to our show-stopping celebration cakes, every item that leaves our kitchen carries the warmth of our passion.

At Bake & Co., you're not just a customer — you're family. Come visit us in Lekki and taste the difference that love makes.
